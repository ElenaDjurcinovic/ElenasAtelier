–About us–

–How it all started–

Elena's Atelier is a dress company that specializes in creating high-end custom-made dresses. Founded by Elena, a skilled designer with years of experience in the fashion industry, our company is known for its attention to detail, quality craftsmanship, and unique designs. Flagship store, located in the heart of a bustling city, is a chic and sophisticated space that reflects the elegance and style of the dresses themselves.

Our atelier offers a wide range of dress styles, from flowing ball gowns to sleek and modern cocktail dresses. We use only the finest fabrics, including silks, satins, and lace, and are known for their exquisite beading and embroidery work. Each dress is carefully crafted to fit the customer's body perfectly, with meticulous attention paid to every detail. Customers can schedule appointments to meet with Elena or one of our talented design associates to discuss their ideas and needs for a custom-made dress.

Elena's Atelier has become a popular choice for those in search of a truly unique and special dress for a wedding, prom, or other special occasions. Personalized service and impeccable craftsmanship have earned us a reputation as one of the top dress companies in the industry. In addition to creating custom dresses, we also offer a selection of ready-to-wear dresses for those who prefer to purchase a dress off the rack.
